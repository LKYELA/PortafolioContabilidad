## Color Palettes and Gradients

### Popular Gradient Combinations
- **Vibrant**: `#667eea` to `#764ba2` (purple-blue)
- **Sunset**: `#f093fb` to `#f5576c` (pink to coral)
- **Ocean**: `#4facfe` to `#00f2fe` (blue to cyan)
- **Forest**: `#43e97b` to `#38f9d7` (green to teal)
- **Fire**: `#fa709a` to `#fee140` (pink to yellow)

### Modern Color Schemes
- **Dark Mode**: Deep blues, purples with neon accents
- **Minimalist**: Monochromatic with one accent color
- **Corporate**: Professional blues and grays
- **Creative**: Vibrant, unexpected combinations
- **Nature-inspired**: Earth tones and natural greens
